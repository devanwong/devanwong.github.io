Hi! I'm Devan.

I am currently an email developer, looking to turn into a web developer. I have been in the email development world for over 5 years, though I love the work that I do, I am ready to take on new challenges.

Growing up.

I grew up in the Bay Area my entire life. I was born in Oakland, raised in Richmond, went to college in San Jose, and now work in Sunnyvale. Growing up, I played sports year round. Started off as a basketball player, then eventually steered towards volleyball. I was a pure definition of a tomboy. When college came around, I know that I wanted to be an art major. With social media becoming more and more popular, I was truly addicted with the way something looks and how it works. So when I went to San Jose State, did a double major, one in graphic design and the other in digital media arts. However, after college, it sort of took a 180 turn, and needed to find a job. I knew I wasn't ready for the tech world, so I took a job on Craigslist for a project manager position. Though this was not a bread winning job, this really taught me some core strengths such as business sales, professional communication and a key component o organization. After a few years as a project manager, I realized that I really wanted to go back to school and learn how to code. I had a small understanding of HTML and CSS thanks to Myspace, but I learn wants to take it to the next level. I knew that going back to college, was not ideal, because of the time. So I decided to invest in enrolling in a bootcamp. This bootcamp, Coding Dojo is a 6-week accelerated bootcamp that teaches you how to become a full-stack developer. After I was done, it took me 6 months before I landed my first job, with Google. Well, a contractor through Google. That's how I became a email developer, and have been doing that ever since.
